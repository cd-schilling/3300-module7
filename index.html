1. Introduction

The video game industry has experienced tremendous growth over the past few decades. Review scores from Metacritic have become an essential indicator of a game's success. By analyzing these scores, we can understand trends in consumer preferences and market dynamics.

This study explores the relationship between video game sales and Metacritic scores, aiming to determine how review scores influence commercial success.

4. Conclusion

The analysis of Metacritic scores and video game sales provides valuable insights into the industry's trends. While high review scores can positively influence sales, other factors such as marketing strategies, brand loyalty, and game genre also play significant roles.

This study highlights the importance of both critical reception and market strategies in determining a game's commercial success. Future research could explore deeper connections between user-generated content, social media impact, and long-term game performance.
